Privacy Policy

Commitment to Privacy

Musafa Al-Qur'an is designed to be your spiritual companion. We believe your relationship with the Holy Qur'an is private. Our application is built with a focus on offline functionality and minimal data collection.

Data Storage

Your reading progress, bookmarks, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not use external databases to track your personal reading habits.

Usage of AI

The AI features (Musafa Companion) use cloud-based models to process voice input and provide ayah continuations. Your voice recordings are processed in real-time and are not stored permanently on our servers or sold to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party APIs to fetch surah data, translations, and audio files. These services may receive standard technical information (such as your IP address) necessary to fulfill the request, but they do not receive any personal identification from our app.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Musafa Al-Qur'an team.
